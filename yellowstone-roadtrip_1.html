Good to Know

Before You Leave

🎫

Yellowstone Entry

Book your entry pass at recreation.gov. Timed entry reservations are sometimes required. A 7-day vehicle pass is ~$35 USD.

🌡️

Mid-May Weather

Shoulder season — fewer crowds but cold nights (can dip near freezing). Layers essential. Snow possible at elevation.

🦬

Wildlife Safety

Stay 25 yards from bison and elk, 100 yards from bears and wolves. Use pullouts and binoculars — animals come to you in Lamar Valley.

🛂

Border Crossing

Have passports ready. Check wait times at Peace Arch / Pacific Highway. NEXUS cards will speed things up both ways.

⛽

Fuel & Cell Service

Fill up when you can — long gaps between stations in Montana. Download offline maps in Google Maps before entering Yellowstone.

🌅

Early Mornings Win

Arrive at major sites before 9am. Old Faithful, Grand Prismatic, and Artist Point all get significantly more crowded by mid-morning.
